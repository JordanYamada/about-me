About Me



Who am I?


Hello. My name is Jordan Yamada. I was born in Portland, OR, but I grew up in Saipan, a small island in the Pacific. I live in the Seattle area now, and I do enjoy the grey skies and the rainy weather.






I will proudly admit to my strong disdain for the onion. This root veggie that plagues our dishes has not place in this world. You can try all you like, but I will not be convinced otherwise. Down with the onions!





My hatred for onions are only balanced by my love for Pumpkin Spice. Coffee, cookies, pie, candles, car scents... I can go on and on. It just evokes that soothing, cozy, Autumn mood. Falling, crunchy leaves, pumpkins decorating the neighborhood, a brisk wind and a warm blanket. What better to bring that back then the scent and taste of pumpkin spice? I'm craving it, already.
